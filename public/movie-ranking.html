The Best Movies According to Maxine

My top three movies of all time

Drive My Car (2021)

A beautifully shot drama piece covering one's understanding of love and coping with grief.

The Matrix Trilogy (1993 - 2003)

An awesome sci-fi series - full of action, 90s vintage tech, and timeless questions around society.

Shrek (2001-2010)

An offbeat spin about an ogre's life journey from being a social hermit to an ogre dad. Full of funny meme-like scenes and medieval fairy-tale characters with a modern twist.
